About

Welcome to Paw Day Care we are a dog walking service covering Leeds and the surrounding areas. We have experienced, insured and fully vetted dog walkers available in the following areas:

  • Leeds
  • Horsforth
  • Chapel Allerton
  • Alwoodley
  • Oakwood

Our family run business was born out of our love for dogs, having been a regular user of dog walkers myself I am all too aware of the difficulty in finding reliable and trustworthy people to look after my beloved dogs. I started my very own walking service two years ago, which has gone from strength to strength, I am very fortunate to have created a job for myself which I truly love!
